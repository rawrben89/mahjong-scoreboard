Win Method Bonuses
自摸 Zimo — Self DrawDrawing your own winning tile from the wall
+1 Fan
All three losers pay you. Each pays full amount.
門清 Concealed GateWinning by discard with a fully concealed hand
+1 Fan
No tiles declared (no called chow/pung). Last tile is from someone's discard.
嶺上開花 Dead Wall DrawDrawing the winning tile from the supplement wall after a kong
+1 Fan
After declaring a kong, the extra draw completes your hand.
海底撈月 Last TileWinning on the very last drawable tile in the wall
+1 Fan
The final tile from the wall (海底牌) completes your hand.
搶槓 Robbing the KongDeclaring a win by stealing an opponent's added kong tile
+1 Fan
When an opponent adds a 4th tile to an existing pung, you can steal it to win if it completes your hand.
